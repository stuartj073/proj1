The faces behind TurnStyle!

Meet Sebastien!

Sebastien has been involved in the bakery industry from a very young age. From cycling around Paris collecting the the rounds of ingredients and mopping and closing down the kitchen to running his very own bakery shop in the suburbs of Paris.

Meet Eloise!

Eloise was also introduced to the kitchen at a very young age. Her mother, originally from Turkey, brought with her a wealth of knowledge from Turkish cuisine, intertwining it with the local French delicacies for a unique take on the infamous French way of eating.
